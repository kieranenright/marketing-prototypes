Creative Format: Hybrid Recommended

Food photography integrated with actual app UI. Highest converting format for food apps.

Lifestyle Photography

Pure food photos, no app UI. Emotional but doesn't show the product. May not comply with Apple's requirement to show actual app UI.

NOT RECOMMENDED

Pure UI Screenshots

Clean device mockups. Shows exact product but feels clinical for food apps. Low emotional engagement, forgettable.

UNDERPERFORMS

Hybrid (Food + UI)

App UI with food photo backgrounds, text overlays, branded framing. Combines appetite appeal with product credibility.

RECOMMENDED ✔

Keyword Strategy

US market keyword targeting

Apple: 100-character keyword field assigned per CPP. Google: keywords embedded in description text.

Primary Keyword Set

recipes,cookbook,meal planning,dinner ideas,meal planner,recipe book,digital cookbook,recime
92 of 100 characters — ready to paste into App Store Connect

Tier 2 — Rotate In

healthy recipes cooking meal prep recipe organizer dinner recipes easy recipes quick meals

Tier 3 — Long-Tail

recipe collection recipe planner cooking ideas recipe box meal ideas food planner dinner planner

Seasonal — Rotate Quarterly

christmas recipes thanksgiving dinner summer recipes bbq recipes holiday cooking easter baking
💡

On misspellings ("cookbok", etc.): Both stores now auto-correct common typos in search. Don't waste keyword characters on misspellings — use legitimate long-tail terms instead. Note: "recime" is included as a competitor brand name, not a misspelling.
