Frequently asked questions

What is the Cookbook Creator Platform?

A permanent home for your recipes inside the HelloFresh app. We convert your existing video content into structured step-by-step recipe cards — searchable, saveable, and accessible to 1M+ users who are actively deciding what to cook. Think of it as the anti-algorithm: your recipes never get buried.

Do I need to create new content?

No. Zero extra work required. We take your existing video recipes and convert them into structured recipe cards with ingredients, steps, and timings. You keep creating on your platforms — we handle the rest.

How is this different from social media saves?

Social saves are where recipes go to die. On Cookbook, every save leads to a cook. Users open recipes in active cooking mode — with step-by-step instructions, timers, and ingredient lists. You get visibility into who actually made your food, not just who double-tapped.

Does it cost anything?

Free platform access during the pilot phase for selected creators. No hidden fees, no revenue share from your other channels.

What are the selection criteria?

We value recipe quality and originality over follower counts. If people watch your content and want to cook what you make, you're exactly who we're looking for.
