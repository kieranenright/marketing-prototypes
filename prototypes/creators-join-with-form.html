FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a Creator Page?

Your Creator Page is your profile inside the HelloFresh app. It showcases your bio, your recipes, and your collections — all in one place. Think of it as a recipe portfolio with a built-in audience of millions of subscribers who are actively looking for what to cook next.

How does it work with my existing website?

Your Creator Page complements rather than replaces your website, with direct linking to external channels and built-in reach to millions of active recipe browsers.

I only have rough recipes and phone photos. Can I still join?

Yes. We use AI tools to structure rough notes into proper recipes and enhance phone photos to publication quality.

How do I make money?

You get paid for what you publish. HelloFresh pays a flat fee for each post and for each recipe published to Creator Recipes. No revenue share, no complicated splits, just a straightforward fee for your content. As your audience grows inside the app, your value to the platform grows with it.

Does it cost anything?

Free platform access during the pilot phase for selected creators. Subscription pricing planned post-pilot.

What are the selection criteria?

Professional quality, originality, and range valued over follower counts. Culinary professionals welcome.
