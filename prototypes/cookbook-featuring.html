New In-App Feature: Cookbook
How It's Helping Users
What featuring would help us achieve
Reach new audiences
The Cookbook is free for all users, even without a subscription. Featuring would introduce it to millions of people who have never used HelloFresh before.
Drive organic engagement
Deepen engagement with existing customers and re-engage former customers with a genuinely useful, free tool.
Establish a daily food companion
Featuring supports our ambition to make the HelloFresh app a daily destination for food inspiration and planning, beyond the weekly meal kit.
The problem
we're solving.
Cooking from a video is frustrating. Meal inspiration is scattered across different social platforms, saved in endless folders, or forgotten altogether. Great ideas rarely make it to the kitchen. People are rewinding, pausing, and replaying with messy hands, scrubbing back to catch an ingredient.

The Cookbook bridges that gap. It allows users to import recipe videos directly from social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok. The app automatically transforms these videos into clear, step-by-step cooking instructions, complete with a detailed ingredient list and a grocery list they can take shopping. It works through the native share sheet, so there's zero friction.

This is part of a bigger shift. HelloFresh is evolving from a meal-kit service into a daily food companion. Your partner for dinner, whether you're cooking a HelloFresh recipe or something you found on TikTok. The Cookbook is available for free to all HelloFresh app users, even without a subscription.
Accessibility
We're committed to making HelloFresh's Cookbook work for every home cook, which is why we have improvements on our roadmap to ensure the feature is inclusive and accessible to all.
