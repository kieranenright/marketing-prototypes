Frequently asked questions

1 How is this different from posting recipes on social? ▼

On social, your recipe competes with every other post for 24–48 hours, then disappears into saved folders that 73% of people never reopen. On HelloFresh, your recipe lives in a Cookbook that people return to when they're deciding what to cook. The audience intent is fundamentally different — they're planning dinner, not scrolling.

2 Do my followers need a HelloFresh subscription? ▼

No. Cookbook is completely free — anyone can download the HelloFresh app and save your recipes without a subscription. This means your entire audience can use it, not just paying customers. No paywall between your content and the people who want to cook it.

3 I only have rough recipes and phone photos. Can I still join? ▼

Yes. We help you structure recipes into clear steps and ingredient lists. Professional photography isn't required — authentic phone photos often outperform studio shots. What matters is that your audience engages with your recipes and wants to cook them.

4 What data will I actually see? ▼

Cook-throughs (how many people actually made your recipe), ratings, repeat cooks, save-to-cook conversion, and collection subscriber growth. For the first time, you'll see beyond impressions to real cooking behaviour — the only metric that matters for food creators.

5 Do I lose ownership of my recipes? ▼

No. Your recipes remain yours. They're attributed to you with your profile, handle, and links back to your social channels. HelloFresh is an additional distribution channel, not a replacement. You can remove any recipe at any time.

6 Who is eligible to join? ▼

Food creators who share original recipes and have an engaged audience. Follower count matters less than engagement — if your audience saves your recipes (even if they never make them), you're exactly who we built this for. 200 pilot spots. Both established creators and emerging voices welcome.
