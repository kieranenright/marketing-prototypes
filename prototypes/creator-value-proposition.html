01 — The Problem

Your recipes get watched,
not cooked.

Creators pour hours into recipe content — developing, filming, editing. It performs well on social: views, likes, saves. But the vast majority of those saves never become meals. The creator-audience relationship ends at the scroll.

48h
Content shelf life
Recipe content has a 48-hour window before the algorithm buries it. Months of work, gone in two days.
0%
Cook-through visibility
You don't know who's actually cooking your food. No data, no feedback loop — just likes.
∞
Saves, never cooked
Audiences bookmark the video, scroll on, and never cook it. Saves feel like engagement. They're not.

The Core Gap

You create recipes to be cooked, but the platform you publish on isn't built for cooking.

02 — The Value Proposition

A permanent home for your recipes.
Where people actually cook them.

HelloFresh Cookbook gives creators a cookable recipe library inside the app where 1M+ people plan their meals every week. Three things creators don't get anywhere else:

1
Permanence
Your recipes live in a searchable, cookable format — not buried in a feed. Structured step-by-step cards, not expiring videos. Your content keeps working long after you've posted it.
2
Reach beyond the algorithm
Get in front of 1M+ active HelloFresh app users who are already planning what to cook — not passively scrolling. Your profile lives in the app, immune to algorithm changes.
3
Earn from recipes that get cooked
Move beyond flat-fee sponsorships. The monetisation model is in development — but the direction is clear: earnings tied to real cooking activity, not just content views. Pilot launching in the US first.

Plus: zero extra work.

We convert your existing video content into structured recipes. You don't create anything new. HelloFresh handles the extraction — you just approve.
