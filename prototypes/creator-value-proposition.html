The Core Problem
Your recipes get watched,
not cooked.
Creators pour hours into recipe content — developing, filming, editing. It performs well on social: views, likes, saves. But the vast majority of those saves become a graveyard. The creator-audience relationship ends at the scroll.
48h

Content Shelf Life

Your recipe content has a 48-hour window before the algorithm buries it. Months of work, gone from the feed in two days.

0%

Cook-Through Visibility

You don’t know who’s actually cooking your food. No data, no feedback loop, no lasting connection with your audience beyond the like button.

∞

Saved, Never Cooked

Your audience bookmarks the video, scrolls on, and never actually cooks it. Saves feel like engagement — but they’re a graveyard of good intentions.

The Gap
You create recipes to be cooked, but the platform you publish on isn’t built for cooking.
