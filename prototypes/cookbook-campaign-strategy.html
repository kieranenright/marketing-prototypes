01
Pain Point
Don't just watch it. Cook it.
"Stop pausing. Stop rewinding. Start cooking."
Social media recipes aren't built for the kitchen. EIs cook 4–6x a week and hit this friction daily — Cookbook turns any video into proper step-by-step instructions.
Insight
EIs find recipes on TikTok and Instagram constantly — but social media recipes aren't built for the kitchen. You pause, rewind, squint at measurements, get flour on your phone, lose your place. For someone who cooks 4–6x a week, this friction is daily. Cookbook turns any video recipe into proper step-by-step instructions.
Tension
The recipe looked effortless in 30 seconds. Then you actually tried to cook it from the video. The gap between how a recipe looks on screen and the reality of following it in your kitchen — that's the pain point Cookbook eliminates.
Content Formats
POV: cooking from a TikTok vs. from Cookbook Split-screen pause-rewind chaos vs. calm step-by-step Expectation vs. reality (relatable frustration) Duets with recipe creators showing the Cookbook version "The recipe said 'a pinch'" UGC moments

02
Achievement
Don't just imagine it. Cook it.
"No backup plan needed. No delivery app on standby."
65% of EIs enjoy preparing food, but even confident cooks hesitate on unfamiliar recipes. Cookbook gives them structured instructions to nail any saved recipe first time.
Insight
EIs are ambitious cooks — 65% enjoy the experience of preparing food, they host, they experiment. But even confident cooks hesitate on unfamiliar recipes. Cookbook gives them structured, reliable instructions that mean they can nail any saved recipe first time. The backup pizza order becomes unnecessary.
Tension
You found the recipe. You bought the ingredients. You're halfway through and something's not right. Do you push through or order takeaway? Cookbook means you never have to make that call — clear instructions, proper timings, every step accounted for.
Content Formats
"I actually made this" transformation reveals Saved recipe → finished dish (before/after) Confidence storytelling: first-time nailing a recipe Creator collabs: cooking something they've been saving for months "Deleted the delivery app" moments

03
Cultural Behaviour
Don't just save it. Cook it.
"Saved 500. Cooked 0. Until now."
EIs use 2.6 planning tools on average and have hundreds of saved recipes they never cook. 44% say they're running out of ideas despite a goldmine of saved inspiration.
Insight
EIs use 2.6 planning tools on average — Instagram saves, TikTok bookmarks, screenshots, notes apps. They've curated hundreds of recipes across platforms but cook almost none. 44% say they're running out of ideas despite having an untapped goldmine of saved inspiration. Cookbook turns the graveyard into a living, cookable collection.
Tension
You save recipes because you genuinely want to cook them. But they pile up across platforms, lose context, and become a graveyard of good intentions. The gap between saving and cooking feels impossible to close — until everything lives in one place with proper instructions.
Content Formats
"Show your saved recipes" scroll-through Saved vs. cooked counter (the uncomfortable truth) "Cooking recipe #247 from my graveyard" Graveyard cleanup challenge — import, organise, cook "Be honest: do you save or actually cook?"

Open Questions
What we need to solve.
These are the blockers and risks we need to address to deliver this campaign successfully.
Organic Social Deployment

How do we deploy the same content across multiple markets and languages?

Organic social channels are currently managed locally per market. We need a unified deployment model that ensures the same content, messaging, and timing is delivered consistently across all markets and languages.

Creative Capacity

Can we produce enough content for this plan?

The campaign is ambitious. We need to confirm creative resources, production timelines, and whether we need external support to deliver at the volume required.

Paid Social Resourcing

How do we secure paid social resources?

We need dedicated paid social support to run, optimise, and scale campaigns across Meta and TikTok. Resourcing and ownership needs to be confirmed.

Visibility

How do we maintain visibility across all channels?

With activity running across organic, paid, and ASO simultaneously, we need a reporting framework that gives us real-time visibility into performance across every channel.
