Key Learnings
1
Static meme/relatable content dominates reach
Consistently 3x+ the reach of video content, with best post hitting 9.9K.
8.1K avg reach 9.9K best post
2
Carousels generate the highest engagement rate and only follower gains
Best format for deeper interaction and profile visits.
0.92% eng rate +1 follow
3
UGC product demo videos need paid support to perform
Feature walkthrough clips are not gaining algorithmic traction organically.
~2.2K avg reach 19 avg interactions
4
Problem framing outperforms feature education
"Never lose a recipe again" framed as solving frustration gets 3x+ the reach of "here's how to save." Humour and self recognition drive shares, not product benefits.
Pain awareness > Feature demo 64 interactions (best)
5
Recruitment CTAs drive highest engagement rate of any format
"Creators Wanted" static hit 1.96% engagement and generated 41 applications (4 contacted). Audience responds strongly to being invited to participate, not just consume.
1.96% eng rate 41 applications 4 contacted 94 interactions
Recommendations:
1. Lead with static/meme formats for reach; boost UGC video with paid.
2. Test more carousels: best engagement rate and only format generating follows.
3. Frame messaging around the problem, not the feature.
